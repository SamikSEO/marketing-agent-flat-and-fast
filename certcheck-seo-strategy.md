# CertCheck — SEO Strategy Skill

## Context

CertCheck solves three fundamentally different problems depending on the audience segment:

| Segment | Core Problem Being Solved |
|---|---|
| **Issuers** (Universities, EdTech, Boards) | Operational burden + security of issuing credentials |
| **Verifiers** (BGV Agencies, Corporate HR, Recruiters) | Credential fraud + slow, expensive verification |
| **Guests** (Students, Job Seekers, Freelancers) | Portability + ownership of their own credentials |

Every piece of SEO content must be written with one primary segment in mind. Mixing messaging across segments in a single page weakens both ranking signal and conversion.

---

## Segment 1 — Issuers (Universities, EdTech, Certification Bodies)

### Pain Points to Address
- Massive manual hours, paper costs, and shipping logistics for physical certificates
- Forged certificates damaging institutional credibility and degree value
- Re-issuing lost certificates to alumni years or decades post-graduation
- Fear that switching to digital/blockchain will break existing LMS or ERP systems (Canvas, Moodle, Banner)

### Keyword Themes

**Commercial Intent (Decision-makers actively shopping):**
- "Digital certificate generation platform"
- "Automated certificate issuance software"
- "API for digital credentials"
- "White-label certificate platform"
- "Bulk certificate issuance tool"

**Problem-Solving / Informational (Early research stage):**
- "How to stop certificate forgery"
- "Blockchain in higher education credentials"
- "Automated certificate software for EdTech"
- "Digital vs physical degree certificates"
- "How to manage alumni certificate requests"

**Integration-Specific (Tech buyer targeting):**
- "Digital certificates for [LMS Name] integration"
- "Blockchain credentials API for Moodle"
- "Canvas LMS digital certificate integration"

### Content Formats That Work
- **How-To Guides:** "How to Safely Issue Bulk Certificates via API"
- **Comparison Pages:** "Physical vs. Digital Degrees: Cost and Security Analysis"
- **Integration Landing Pages:** One page per LMS/ERP integration, keyword-optimized
- **Case Studies:** Institution saved X% in costs after switching to blockchain credentials
- **ROI Calculators:** Cost of manual certificate issuance vs. digital automation

### Content Angle
Lead with **cost reduction and operational efficiency** first. Fraud prevention is the secondary hook. Decision-makers at universities are under budget pressure — the operational savings argument opens the door; the fraud/reputation risk argument closes it.

## Segment 2 — Verifiers (BGV Agencies, Corporate HR, Recruiters)

### Pain Points to Address
- Waiting weeks for universities to respond to email/mail verification requests
- High per-check costs with legacy BGV networks
- Resume padding and fake degrees slipping through basic filters
- Inability to scale manual verification during bulk hiring seasons (campus placements)

### Keyword Themes

**Commercial / Transactional (High buying intent):**
- "Instant background verification API"
- "Automated degree verification software"
- "Bulk candidate vetting platform"
- "Pre-employment credential check tool"
- "Employer background check service India"

**Pain-Point Driven (Problem-aware, solution-seeking):**
- "How to detect fake degrees"
- "Reduce background check turnaround time"
- "Employment verification automation"
- "How to verify educational certificates online"
- "Fake certificate detection software"

### Content Formats That Work
- **Industry-Specific Landing Pages:** "Credential Verification for IT & Tech Recruitment" / "Automated Vetting for BGV Agencies"
- **Interactive Tools:** "Cost of Employee Mis-hire Calculator" / "BGV Turnaround Time Calculator" — link-bait format, drives backlinks
- **MoFu Guides:** "The Recruiter's Guide to Detecting Digital Certificate Fraud in 2026"
- **Comparison Pages:** "Manual BGV vs. Blockchain Verification: Speed, Cost, Accuracy"
- **Stat-led thought leadership:** Articles anchored in India-specific hiring fraud data

### Content Angle
Lead with **speed and cost**. Verifiers are volume-driven — they care about TAT (turnaround time) and cost per check before they care about blockchain. Introduce blockchain as the mechanism that enables the speed and reliability they already want. Don't lead with blockchain; lead with the outcome.

## Segment 3 — Guests (Students, Job Seekers, Freelancers)

### Pain Points to Address
- Misplacing physical certificates or losing access to old college email accounts where digital PDFs were sent
- Proving credential legitimacy to clients or employers without a painful back-and-forth verification loop
- Difficulty embedding or showcasing verified credentials on LinkedIn, CVs, or personal websites

### Keyword Themes

**User-Centric / High Volume:**
- "How to add certificates to LinkedIn"
- "Secure digital locker for certificates"
- "How to verify my own degree on blockchain"
- "Shareable digital resume link"
- "How to build a verifiable CV"
- "Digital credential wallet India"

### Content Formats That Work
- **Optimized Help Center Articles:** "How to Share Your CertCheck Credentials on LinkedIn (Step-by-Step)"
- **SEO-Optimized Public Verification Pages:** Every public certificate link CertCheck generates should be a clean, branded, Google-indexable page — if someone searches a certificate ID or candidate name, CertCheck's verification page should surface
- **Programmatic SEO Play:** Each verified certificate creates a unique, indexable URL — this scales organic footprint automatically with every new certificate issued

### Content Angle
This segment drives **volume and network effect**, not direct revenue. Treat Guest content as top-of-funnel brand building. The goal is: students share CertCheck links → employers click them → employers discover the platform → employers become Verifier clients.

## Core SEO Action Plan

### Homepage & Pillar Page Keywords
- "Blockchain Digital Credential Platform"
- "Automated Certificate Verification Solution"
- "Blockchain-based certificate verification India"

### The "Vs" Content Strategy
Create comparison content targeting competitors and legacy approaches:
- "CertCheck vs. [Competitor Name]"
- "Blockchain Certificates vs. Secured PDFs"
- "Digital Verification vs. Manual BGV: A 2026 Comparison"

### The Tech Stack Play
Build dedicated sub-pages for every technical integration CertCheck supports.
IT decision-makers search specifically for tools that fit their existing systems:
- "CertCheck + Workday integration"
- "CertCheck + Zapier"
- "CertCheck + Canvas LMS"
- "CertCheck API documentation"

### Content Production Priority Order
1. Verifier segment — highest commercial intent, fastest path to revenue
2. Issuer segment — supply-side moat, creates the credential inventory Verifiers need
3. Guest segment — volume play, brand amplification, programmatic SEO

---

## SEO Rules for All CertCheck Content

- Every article targets **1 primary keyword** + 3–5 semantic/LSI variations
- H1 must include the primary keyword naturally (not forced)
- **FAQ section is mandatory** on every blog — optimized for featured snippets and Google AI Overviews
- All statistics must be sourced and no older than 18 months
- Minimum lengths: Blog = 700 words | Pillar/Guide = 1,500 words
- Internal linking: minimum 2 suggestions per piece
- Schema markup: Article + FAQPage on every blog; Product schema on feature/landing pages
- Reading level target: Grade 10–12 (clear and professional, not academic)
